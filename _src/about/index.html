About Little Tinker

At Little Tinker we build meaningful experiences and bring these stories to life with intelligent electronics that interact with online events. Our products, strive to reach one goal create a meaningful and magical experience for our audience that gives them a story to share.

 

We tell our creative friends we are engineers.

We tell our developer friends we are artists.

And we tell our parents not to worry.

And we ask ourselves, "How can we change the world?"
